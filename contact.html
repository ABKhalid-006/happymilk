From Hasilpur Road:

  1. Enter GECHS through Gate No. 2 and proceed straight ahead.
  2. At the end of the road, take a right turn.
  3. Take the first left and continue straight.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship your products?

Currently, we only offer local delivery within a 25-mile radius of our farm. We're working on expanding our shipping options in the future.

Can I visit the farm without a tour?

Our farm store is open to visitors during regular business hours. However, access to the barns and production facilities is only available through our guided tours.

Are your products organic?

While we follow many organic practices, we are not currently certified organic. We do prioritize sustainable farming methods and avoid the use of antibiotics and hormones.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer wholesale options for restaurants, cafes, and grocery stores. Please contact us at wholesale@happymilk.com for more information.
