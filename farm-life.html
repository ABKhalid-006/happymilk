Farm Life

Take a peek into our daily operations and see how we care for our animals and produce the dairy products you love.

A Day on the Farm

Life on Happy Milk Farm starts early. Our day begins at 5:00 AM when our team arrives to milk the cows. We use modern, hygienic milking equipment that’s gentle on the cows and helps us deliver the highest quality milk.

Since our cows are kept in well-ventilated sheds, they are provided with a carefully balanced, organic diet made up of hay, silage, and natural supplements — ensuring they stay healthy and well-nourished throughout the day.

Our team works around the clock to monitor the cows’ health, maintain clean and comfortable living spaces, and manage milk processing in our on-site creamery, where it’s turned into fresh dairy products.

In the evening, the cows are milked again before resting in their clean, calm sheds — ready for another peaceful day on the farm.

Our Cows

At Happy Milk, our herd consists primarily of Jersey cows, chosen for their rich, high-quality milk.

Each cow is treated as an individual — with her own name, personality, and specific needs. Our dedicated team provides hands-on, personalized care for every member of the herd.

We believe that happy cows produce better milk, which is why we ensure they enjoy:

  • Clean, comfortable sheds with plenty of space to move and rest
  • A balanced, organic diet consisting of hay, silage, and natural supplements
  • Regular veterinary check-ups to ensure their health and well-being
  • Gentle, respectful handling and a calm, low-stress environment
